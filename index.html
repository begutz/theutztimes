BREAKING NEWS: Chicken sandwich treats are the suspected culprit of tummy aches in Amy the beagle

You might think that it was the peanuts or cottage cheese which caused the beagle's upset stomach. But most recent studies show that the beagle most likely got the runs due to the "chicken sandwich treats" that Michael bought.

After eating copious amounts of grass, Amy was able to feel better and continue her daily program of taking naps, growling at neighbor's dogs and expecting pets from family members. Now the question is, are we still going to feed her the chicken sandwich treats?

Crazy rains in Helsinki on July 16, 2019!

There was some crazy rain along with strong winds in Vuosaari this afternoon. Residents from the Utz family stayed indoors to stay dry. Later in the day, rain stopped and Amy was able to go outside and sniff for bunnies.

So far, summer in Helsinki has been amazing. Most days have been sunny but not scorching hot. Days are VERY long, with sunset at 22:30.
